The Revolutionization of Transportation
Site Author
 Anthony Arphan


Abstract
 The article on Common Good Ventures discusses the revolutionary impact of computers on transportation.  It highlights the significant advancements made through technologies like GPS and autonomous vehicles.  GPS has transformed navigation by providing real-time directions, improving efficiency, and reducing traffic.  Additionally, the development of autonomous vehicles, driven by computers, promises to enhance safety, reduce traffic congestion, and optimize fuel consumption.  The article also covers the role of Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) in managing traffic and improving transportation efficiency.

 Improvements in Transportation Throughout History
Site Author
 Histopedia
Abstract
 The article on Histopedia discusses the evolution of transportation from the pre-industrial era to the present and beyond.  It outlines key milestones, such as the introduction of walking, horseback riding, and early boats in ancient times, to the development of steam locomotives, automobiles, airplanes, and modern technologies like autonomous vehicles and hyperloop systems.  The piece also examines the impact of transportation innovations on society, highlighting increased mobility, economic growth, environmental concerns, and advancements in sustainability.  Additionally, it looks at future trends like space exploration and sustainable transportation technologies.
